# Brand voice starter

Copy the relevant block into `MASTER_CONTEXT.md` and fill in your details.

---

## Template

```markdown
## Brand

- **Tone:** [e.g. warm and relatable / clinical and premium / bold and playful]
- **Audience:** [e.g. women 25–40 interested in clean skincare]
- **Words to use:** [e.g. glow, ritual, effortless]
- **Words to avoid:** [e.g. anti-aging, chemicals, cheap]
- **Visual aesthetic:** [e.g. soft natural light, earthy tones, minimalist backgrounds]
- **Never show:** [e.g. clutter, bold logos, aggressive poses]
```

---

## Example: Clean skincare brand

```markdown
## Brand

- **Tone:** Warm, honest, science-adjacent. Like a knowledgeable friend, not a lab.
- **Audience:** Women 25–38, ingredient-conscious, tired of overpriced routines.
- **Words to use:** glow, ritual, simple, real, effective, skin barrier, hydration.
- **Words to avoid:** anti-aging, chemicals, flawless, perfect skin, pore-free.
- **Visual aesthetic:** Soft morning light, cream and sage color palette, uncluttered countertops, dewy skin.
- **Never show:** Heavy makeup, harsh shadows, glossy magazine look, text overlays.
```

## Example: DTC fitness supplement brand

```markdown
## Brand

- **Tone:** Motivating but grounded. No hype. Real people, real results.
- **Audience:** Men and women 20–35, gym-goers, functional fitness crowd.
- **Words to use:** performance, recovery, fuel, clean, training, gains.
- **Words to avoid:** shredded, ripped, extreme, transformation, before/after.
- **Visual aesthetic:** Gritty gym lighting, natural sweat, athletic wear, product in action.
- **Never show:** Photoshopped bodies, steroids-adjacent imagery, overcrowded frame.
```

## Example: Lifestyle content creator

```markdown
## Brand

- **Tone:** Authentic, a little unfiltered. Creator-energy, not brand-speak.
- **Audience:** Gen Z and younger Millennials who consume creator content daily.
- **Words to use:** honestly, real talk, obsessed, lowkey, vibe.
- **Words to avoid:** luxury, exclusive, premium, sophisticated.
- **Visual aesthetic:** iPhone aesthetic, natural chaos in background, authentic expressions, UGC selfie format.
- **Never show:** Over-styled sets, staged smiles, text overlays, brand logos.
```
