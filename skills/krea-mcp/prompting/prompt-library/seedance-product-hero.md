# Product Hero — Seedance

**Use when:** A dramatic, no-person product showcase. The product is shot like a movie poster — moody lighting, colored backdrop, and elemental interaction (water splash, ice, mist, sparks, smoke). Best for beverages, supplements, cans, bottles, cosmetics, tech gadgets.

**Read first:** [seedance.md](seedance.md) for platform rules and the adaptation checklist.

---

## What defines this style

1. **The product is the hero.** Every shot framed around the product — extreme close-ups, dramatic angles, hero compositions. Camera treats the product the way a portrait photographer treats a face.
2. **Elemental interaction creates the action.** No person — visual energy comes from the environment: water splashing against the can, rain falling on the surface, mist swirling, ice cracking. Product stays mostly static while the world moves.
3. **Dramatic lighting and color set the mood.** Single deep-color background (blue, black, teal, amber), spot-lit product, high contrast.

---

## The structure

```
 1. FORMAT HEADER     — duration, visual style, camera type
 2. PRODUCT           — what it looks like, how it's positioned
 3. ENVIRONMENT       — backdrop color, surface, elemental effects
 4. SHOT SEQUENCE     — visual beats: angles, movements, compositions
 5. TEXT OVERLAYS     — tagline, CTA, feature callouts
 6. TECHNICAL QUALITY — lighting, camera movement, color grade
```

## Layer-by-layer formula

### Layer 1: Format header

```
15 seconds {{CONTENT_TYPE}} video, {{CAMERA_STYLE}}, {{MOOD_DESCRIPTOR}}.
```

| Variable | Options |
|----------|---------|
| `CONTENT_TYPE` | product hero, beverage commercial, premium product showcase, brand film |
| `CAMERA_STYLE` | slow-motion macro photography, dramatic product cinematography, high-speed product photography |
| `MOOD_DESCRIPTOR` | dark and dramatic, moody and premium, bold and energetic, clean and minimal |

---

### Layer 2: Product

```
The @(img1) ({{PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION}}) — {{SURFACE_DETAILS}}, {{CONDITION_DETAILS}}.
```

Be very specific — this is the only thing on screen. Describe label color, shape, material, finish.

---

### Layer 3: Environment

```
Set against a {{BACKDROP}} on a {{SURFACE}}.
{{ELEMENTAL_EFFECT_1}}, {{ELEMENTAL_EFFECT_2}}.
```

**Element bank by product type:**

| Product type | Primary element | Secondary element |
|-------------|----------------|------------------|
| **Beverage / can / bottle** | water splash, rain, pour | condensation, ice, droplets |
| **Supplement / powder** | powder explosion, dust cloud | particles catching light |
| **Skincare / cosmetic** | cream swirl, liquid drip, mist | dewy droplets, refraction |
| **Tech / gadget** | sparks, light trails, electricity | reflections, lens flare |
| **Food** | steam, sizzle, drip | condensation, crumbs |

---

### Layer 4: Shot sequence

3–4 shots escalating in drama: tight/tactile → wide/heroic.

**Shot type bank:**

| Shot type | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Extreme close-up / macro | Opens the video — texture sells quality |
| Grab / interaction | Just a hand — creates scale |
| Dramatic angle | Product from below or tilted — larger than life |
| Hero composition | Product centered, full label visible — the money shot |
| Slow-motion splash | Pure visual spectacle |
| CTA frame | Hero comp with text overlays |

**15-second frameworks:**

| Type | Shot 1 (~3s) | Shot 2 (~3s) | Shot 3 (~4s) | Shot 4 (~5s) |
|------|-------------|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| **Escalating drama** | Macro close-up | Hand grab + splash | Dramatic low angle + rain | Hero comp + tagline |
| **Reveal build** | Blurred product | Focus pulls to label | Splash/pour moment | Multi-variant hero |
| **Pure spectacle** | Slow-mo splash | Dramatic angle, rain | Quick cut to variant | Hero lineup + tagline |

---

### Layer 5: Text overlays

| Timing | What | Style |
|--------|------|-------|
| Mid-video (~8–10s) | Brand tagline or product claim | Large, bold, centered |
| End card (~12–15s) | CTA + feature callouts | "Available at [retailer]" |

**Tagline rules:** Short, punchy — 4–8 words. Bold sans-serif or gothic, white or gold on dark background.

---

### Layer 6: Technical quality

- **Lighting:** High contrast, dramatic shadows. Product is the brightest thing in frame.
- **Camera:** Tack-sharp focus on label. Slow-motion on splash effects. Very smooth, no shake.
- **Color:** Deep saturated backdrop, neutral product tones — product pops against environment.
- **Audio:** Dramatic music bed with foley effects (ice cracking, water splashing). No voice, no dialogue.

[00:00] {{OPENING_SHOT}} — {{MACRO_DETAIL}}.

[00:03] {{SHOT_2}} — {{ACTION_2}}. Text: "{{TAGLINE}}" appears
{{TEXT_TIMING}}.

[00:08] {{SHOT_3}} — {{HERO_MOMENT}}. {{ELEMENTAL_CLIMAX}}.

[00:12] {{CLOSING_FRAME}} — full product visible, {{CTA_TEXT}}.

## Example: Beverage can launch

```
15 seconds beverage commercial, slow-motion macro photography, dark
and dramatic.

The @(img1) (midnight blue aluminum energy drink can — matte finish,
bold silver logo, 12oz) — condensation droplets covering the surface,
ice cold straight from the freezer. Set against a deep midnight blue
gradient background on a wet dark reflective surface. Water splashing
dramatically around the can, water droplets suspended in mid-air,
light refracting through droplets.

[00:00] Extreme close-up of condensation on the can surface, water
droplet sliding down the label in slow motion.

[00:03] The can rises from a pool of water, splash exploding outward
in slow motion. Text snaps on: "STAY COLD." bold white, centered.

[00:08] Dramatic low-angle hero shot — rain falling onto the can,
label fully visible. Text slides in: "No Crash. No Compromise."

[00:12] Hero composition — can centered on wet surface, full label
visible, reflection below. Text: "MIDNIGHT ENERGY COMPANY" in
clean sans-serif. Camera slowly pushes in.

The lighting is dramatic rim lighting catching the silver logo and
can edges. Camera movement is slow and smooth. Color grade: deep blue
backdrop, neutral true-to-life product colors. No dialogue — only
foley effects: water splashing, ice crackling, can cracking open.
```

## Adaptation checklist

- [ ] 15 seconds — full duration for this style
- [ ] No person — product is the only subject
- [ ] No dialogue — text-and-visuals only
- [ ] Elemental interaction specified (primary + secondary)
- [ ] Shot sequence has 3–4 beats with timestamps
- [ ] At least one text overlay (tagline + CTA)
- [ ] Lighting described as dramatic, product spot-lit
- [ ] Camera movement described as slow and smooth
- [ ] Word count 100–260 words
- [ ] No forbidden words (use "dramatic"/"premium" instead of "cinematic")
