# Premium Product Reveal — Seedance

**Use when:** You need a dramatic, dark-background product launch video. No person on screen. The product floats in a black void with dramatic lighting, animated text reveals, and premium material close-ups.

**Read first:** [seedance.md](seedance.md) for platform rules and the adaptation checklist.

---

## What defines this style

Pure black background — no environment, no lifestyle, no person. The product emerges from darkness through dramatic lighting. Text builds phrase by phrase in clean bold typography. Pacing is slow, deliberate, and weighty. Every camera movement is controlled. Every text reveal is timed to land.

---

## The structure

```
 1. VOID STAGE        — black backdrop and lighting setup
 2. PRODUCT HERO      — what the product looks like and how it's lit
 3. TEXT NARRATIVE    — story told through animated typography
 4. REVEAL SEQUENCE   — choreography of product angles and details
 5. VARIANT SHOWCASE  — size/color comparisons (optional)
 6. BRAND CLOSE       — final product name + branding card
```

## Layer-by-layer formula

### Layer 1: Void stage

```
15 seconds premium product reveal video. Pure black background,
{{LIGHTING_STYLE}}, {{LIGHTING_DIRECTION}}.
```

**Lighting options:**
- `dramatic studio rim lighting` — outlines the product against the void (default)
- `soft gradient spotlight` — centered glow, softer feel
- `hard directional key light` — sharp shadows, high contrast
- `warm amber accent lighting` — warmer, heritage/luxury feel
- `cool silver edge lighting` — tech/modern feel

**Lighting direction options:**
- `light coming from above and behind the product`
- `side-lit from the left with a subtle fill on the right`
- `overhead spot with sharp falloff`

**Key rule:** Never describe a visible light source (no lamps, windows, softboxes). The light simply exists — source is invisible.

### Layer 2: Product hero

```
A {{PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION}} — {{MATERIAL_SURFACE}}, {{LIGHT_INTERACTION}}.
The product @(img1) is centered in frame, {{SCALE_CUE}}.
```

**Material-light pairings:**

| Material | Best lighting | Example |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| Brushed metal | Rim light catching edges, grain visible | stainless steel bottle, aluminum case |
| Polished/chrome | Sharp reflections sliding across surface | sunglasses, chrome hardware |
| Matte plastic/rubber | Soft diffused highlights, no hard reflections | phone case, matte bottle |
| Glass/clear | Light refracting, caustic patterns | perfume bottle, glass jar |
| Fabric/textile | Subtle texture under raking light | shoe upper, bag material |

### Layer 3: Text narrative

```
Bold white text appears: "{{LINE_1}}" — {{LINE_1_TIMING}}.
{{LINE_2_TRANSITION}}: "{{LINE_2}}" — {{LINE_2_TIMING}}.
{{LINE_3_TRANSITION}}: "{{LINE_3}}" — {{LINE_3_TIMING}}.
```

**Text reveal timing:**
- `fades in over 1 second` — smooth, premium (opening line, brand name)
- `snaps on screen` — impact, emphasis (key claim)
- `types on letter by letter` — building tension (feature descriptions)
- `slides in from left/right/below` — motion energy (secondary info)

**Text narrative structures:**

| Structure | Line 1 | Line 2 | Line 3 |
|-----------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Introduction** | "INTRODUCING" | Product category claim | Product name |
| **Superlative** | Bold claim ("Our most X ever") | Proof point | Product name |
| **Feature stack** | Feature 1 | Feature 2 | Feature 3 + name |

**Rules:**
- Max 3 text lines in 15 seconds
- Each line under 8 words — billboard, not paragraph
- Text appears center frame or upper third — never very bottom

### Layer 4: Reveal sequence

```
{{OPENING_REVEAL}}. {{CAMERA_MOVE_1}}.
{{TRANSITION}} — {{CAMERA_MOVE_2}}, {{DETAIL_FOCUS}}.
{{FINAL_POSITION}}.
```

**Opening reveal styles:**
- **Rise from below** — product slowly rises into frame (bottles, fire pits)
- **Fade from dark** — lighting gradually illuminates product already in frame (most dramatic)
- **Rotate in** — product spins into position from off-angle
- **Zoom out** — starts on extreme close-up, pulls back to reveal full product

**Camera movements (all must be SLOW):**
- `slow 360 rotation around the product` — shows all sides, premium feel
- `gentle push-in from medium to close-up` — builds intimacy
- `overhead top-down view descending` — interesting top profile
- `slow pan across surface detail` — highlights material quality

## Beat structure (15-second format)

| Beat | Timing | What happens | Text |
|------|--------|-------------|------|
| **1: Tease** | 0–4s | Product emerging from darkness | "INTRODUCING" or bold claim |
| **2: Reveal** | 4–10s | Full product visible, camera moves | Product category + key differentiator |
| **3: Close** | 10–15s | Variant lineup or final hero angle | Full product name + brand |

## Adaptation checklist

- [ ] 15 seconds — full duration for this style
- [ ] Pure black background — void stage, no environment
- [ ] No dialogue — text-and-visuals only
- [ ] Max 3 text lines — each under 8 words, reveal timing specified
- [ ] 2–3 camera movements, all SLOW
- [ ] Brand close included
- [ ] Timestamps covering full 15 seconds
- [ ] Word count 100–260 words
- [ ] No forbidden words (use "dramatic"/"premium" instead of "cinematic")
